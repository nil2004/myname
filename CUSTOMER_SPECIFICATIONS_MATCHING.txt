🔍 KEYWORD EXTRACTION:
═══════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════

Customer Input:
"I want outdoor seating with live music and vegetarian menu"

Step 1: Convert to lowercase
→ "i want outdoor seating with live music and vegetarian menu"

Step 2: Remove punctuation
→ "i want outdoor seating with live music and vegetarian menu"

Step 3: Split into words
→ ["i", "want", "outdoor", "seating", "with", "live", "music", 
    "and", "vegetarian", "menu"]

Step 4: Remove stop words (common words like "i", "want", "and")
→ ["outdoor", "seating", "live", "music", "vegetarian", "menu"]

Step 5: Filter short words (keep 3+ characters)
→ ["outdoor", "seating", "live", "music", "vegetarian", "menu"]

🎯 MATCHING LOGIC:
═══════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════

For each vendor, the AI:

1. Extracts keywords from customer specifications
   ↓
2. Searches vendor description (case-insensitive)
   - Awards 2 points per keyword match
   - Maximum 8 points
   ↓
3. Searches vendor tags (case-insensitive)
   - Awards 2.5 points per keyword match
   - Maximum 8 points
   ↓
4. Searches vendor portfolio highlights (case-insensitive)
   - Awards 2 points per keyword match
   - Maximum 5 points
   ↓
5. Adds specification score to total score
   ↓
6. Ranks vendors by total score
   ↓
7. Selects highest-scoring vendor per category

🔧 STOP WORDS FILTERING:
═══════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════

The AI automatically removes common words that don't add meaning:

Stop Words (Ignored):
├── Articles: "a", "an", "the"
├── Conjunctions: "and", "or", "but"
├── Prepositions: "in", "on", "at", "to", "from"
├── Pronouns: "i", "we", "my", "our"
├── Common verbs: "want", "need", "looking", "like"
└── Others: "is", "are", "was", "will", "can", "should"

Why?
- These words appear in every vendor profile
- They don't help distinguish vendors
- Removing them improves match accuracy
