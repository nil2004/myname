SUPPORT & TROUBLESHOOTING

Common Issues:

✅ Solution: Run the database migration script

❌ Images not showing on customer site
✅ Solution: Ensure vendor is marked as "Verified"

❌ Upload button disabled
✅ Solution: Wait for current upload to complete

❌ "File too large"
✅ Solution: Compress image to under 50MB
